AI Performance Prediction

Our core AI engine analyzes historical employee data to predict future performance trends. It evaluates multiple data points to provide actionable insights.

  • Trend Analysis: Predicts productivity fluctuations based on historical task completion rates.
  • Quality Scoring: Uses machine learning to evaluate the quality of work relative to benchmarks.
  • Attrition Risk: Identifies employees who may be at risk of leaving based on engagement levels.
Role Recommendation Engine

The system intelligently matches employee skills with project requirements and potential career paths.

  • Smart Assignment: Recommends the best team members for a task based on their validated skills.
  • Skill Gap Analysis: Identifies missing skills in a team and suggests relevant training programs.
  • Promotion AI: Highlights high-performers ready for the next level in their career.
ATS Resume Analyzer

PulseHR includes an AI-driven Applicant Tracking System (ATS) that streamlines the hiring process.

  • Automated Scoring: Instantly scores resumes against job descriptions using NLP.
  • Keyword Extraction: Automatically identifies key skills and experience levels from PDF/Word documents.
  • Improvement Suggestions: Provides feedback to applicants on how to optimize their profiles.
